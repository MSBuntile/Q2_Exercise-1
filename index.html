Agape



God's love is beacon, it is a high tower.

His love is a lamp that will shine through your darkest hour.

God's love is like the sun shining in its full strength.

His love is unconditional when measured in length.

God's love is gentle and light, but can never be shaken.

God's love can not be brought with silver or gold.

His love is free and never sold.

God's love is strong and full of life.

His love dries all the tears of strife.

God's love has the power to save .

Surely you can see God's love has conquered the grave.
